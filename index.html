Weeblys Bakery since 1979

Step into Weeblys Bakery and you’ll discover a delicious array of baked goods, from breads and rolls to decadent desserts. Planning to celebrate something special? Our talented, highly trained team considers every detail with the utmost care, from taking your order to the icing on the cake.

  • 1979 – Opened first store, a Thriftway Market in Cottage Grove.
    1980s & 90s – Acquired more stores and operated under the Price Chopper name.
  • 2000 – Began conversion to Market of Choice name & image in response to customers asking for choices in conventional & natural products.
  • 2006 – Opened two stores: one on Willakenzie Road in Eugene, followed by the West Linn store.
  • 2010 – Opened store in Corvallis.
    2012 – Unveiled new central kitchen/distribution center in Eugene, paving the way for growth.
  • 2015 – Opened new store in the Cedar Mill neighborhood of Portland.
    2016 – Opened new store in Bend, named one of the Best Grocery Stores of Central Oregon.
  • 2017 – Opened new store in SE Portland.
    2021 – Opened new store in Medford.
